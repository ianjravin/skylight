What makes Zaka the investment platform of choice

Our Vision

Zaka International’s vision is to see growth oriented SME’s scale up to global corporate businesses and driving African economic growth rate to double-digits.

Our Mission

Zaka International’s mission is to leverage the wealth of Africa to grow and scale Small and Medium sized Enterprises through access to growth resources.
