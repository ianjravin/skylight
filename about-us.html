Welcome to Zaka

For a long time African entrepreneurs, potential wealth creators, have operated in a hostile environment created by commercial banks, microfinance institutions and savings and credit cooperative organisations. Many small and medium-size enterprises (SMEs) are denied capital or subjected to credit at very high interest rates Zaka International is an online marketplace where African entrepreneurs and angel investors interact directly on deals that will favour of business growth and profit for both parties. The investors and entrepreneurs have the power to dictate terms and overcome the barriers to capital access.

Our core business is to find, evaluate, rate, present growth-ready enterprises to access capital from a network of impact investors in the platform. We provide an ecosystem based on trust for ordinary professionals, business executives and entrepreneurs with savings and disposable income to invest directly in SMEs. Small businesses success is the most sustainable way to unlock Africa’s wealth and deliver the much needed development. Read more to understand how you can join the Zaka community and launch your way to unlocking the wealth of Africa.

Growth

Banks ridden with heavy overheads dictate the terms and conditions for both borrowers and investors (savers) to sustain themselves and report high margins, all these to the detriment of the business community. The small businesses have been relegated to the periphery with no access to the much needed finance and support for growth. Commercial banking as we know it today will undergo disruption.

There are clear trends that point to this direction; first, the technological explosion and growth of mobile money has already started changing commercial banking as was witnessed in Kenya with emergence of Mpesa. There are a lot more people who no longer see the need to visit a bank branch as long as their Mpesa is loaded. The second trend is the growth of big data and superior analytics that render the manual credit appraisal irrelevant. Thirdly, the change in demography with emergence of millennials who are no longer familiar with traditional commercial banks points to this disruption.

Our peer-to-peer business platform

There lacks investment opportunities for small–scale investors, even commercial banks do not pay interest of deposits falling below US$235. This leaves the poor locked out of both investments and access to finance. Zaka.co.ke will o er impact-investors and social investors an opportunity to invest short term, by accessing zaka.co.ke website and selecting a deal to invest in, either in part or in whole. Zaka is the micro-investment platform for small scale individuals (peers) and businesses who are excluded from investment opportunities owing to the small amounts of their funds they hold.
